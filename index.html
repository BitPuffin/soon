About

The Lonely Byte is a very small art producing company with a major focus on interactive experiences (Games).

The goal is to produce meaningful experiences regardless if they appeal to everybody. I realize the fact that games has a way of immersing you in a way that no other medium can - and I want to explore that.

If The Lonely Byte becomes successful enough, I will start looking to publish and fund developers who share similar goals regarding games. Creations that change the way we think of games.

There are a lot of ideas and plans to where I want to go with this project/company, it is what I want to devote my life to. I hope I can reach out to you through my creations and give you something of value.
